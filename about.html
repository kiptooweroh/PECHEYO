About Pecheyo Church

Our Doctrine

Pecheyo Church stands firmly on the foundation of the Holy Scriptures. We believe in:

  • The Holy Trinity — Father, Son & Holy Spirit
  • Salvation through Jesus Christ alone
  • Baptism by water and the Holy Spirit
  • The authority of the Bible as the inspired Word of God
  • Evangelism, holiness and discipleship
  • Second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ
